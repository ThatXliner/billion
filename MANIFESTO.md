# Billion

> Congressional App Challenge 2025 -> AI.gov 2026

An AI-powered mobile app that makes political information accessible through engaging, short-form content.

## What is Billion?

Billion transforms complex political topics into digestible, TikTok-style content. The app uses AI to generate summaries, analyses, and visual content about laws, bills, political actions, and their real-world consequences across the political spectrum.
